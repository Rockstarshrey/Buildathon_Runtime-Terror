I have built a web app called "KisanSetu" — a farmer-focused platform for India that provides mandi prices, crop advisory, government schemes, and community features.

Your task is to completely redesign and enhance the UI/UX to make it:
- Extremely intuitive for low-tech users (farmers)
- Mobile-first and responsive
- Clean, modern, and emotionally warm
- High accessibility (large touch targets, readable fonts, multilingual support)
- Fast and lightweight

---------------------------------------

🔷 CURRENT FEATURES:
- Dashboard with greeting, weather, quick actions
- Mandi Prices (cards with crop prices)
- Crop Advisory (stage-based tips)
- Government Schemes (currently empty/basic)
- Community Groups
- Sidebar navigation

---------------------------------------

---------------------------------------

  - Add icons + forecast (next 3 days)

  - Price trends
