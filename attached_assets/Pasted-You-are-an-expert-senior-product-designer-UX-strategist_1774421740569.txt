You are an expert senior product designer + UX strategist + frontend design systems specialist. Your task is to completely redesign and elevate the UI/UX of an existing web app called “KisanSetu”, a farmer-focused platform for Indian users. The current interface already includes these modules: Dashboard, Mandi Prices, Crop Advisory, Government Schemes, Community, and Messages. The app is primarily intended for mobile-first usage, but should also look premium and highly usable on tablet and desktop.

Your goal is NOT to change the core product idea. Your goal is to transform the experience into a polished, modern, trustworthy, accessible, emotionally warm, and practical agricultural platform that feels production-ready, investor-demo-ready, and hackathon-winning.

====================================================
1. PRODUCT CONTEXT
====================================================

Brand name: KisanConnect
Meaning: A digital bridge for farmers
Target audience:
- Indian farmers, including semi-urban and rural users
- Users with varying digital literacy
- Many users may prefer Hindi/regional language support
- Users may access the app on lower-end smartphones, but judges/investors may view on laptop/desktop

Primary user goals:
- Quickly view mandi prices
- Get crop-specific advisory
- Discover government schemes
- Join community discussions
- Send and receive messages
- Feel supported, informed, and empowered

Design feeling:
- Trustworthy
- Warm
- Human
- Grounded in agriculture
- Clean and modern
- Not overly corporate
- Easy for first-time users
- Premium enough for judges and stakeholders

====================================================
2. CURRENT PROBLEMS TO SOLVE
====================================================

The current UI has these issues:
- Too much empty white space on several pages
- Some sections look incomplete or broken when content is low
- The sidebar is visually heavy and occupies too much width
- Content hierarchy is weak
- Cards are repetitive and not visually differentiated enough
- Pages like Government Schemes and Community feel unfinished/blank
- Mobile responsiveness needs major improvement
- The app lacks delight, depth, and refined visual rhythm
- Important content does not pop enough
- The design feels more like a quick prototype than a polished product
- Need stronger CTA structure, clearer spacing, better information density, and improved readability
- Need a more cohesive design system across all screens
- Need better bilingual handling (English + Hindi labels)

====================================================

Create a complete UI/UX enhancement plan and visual redesign direction with the following qualities:

- Modern SaaS-quality interface, but adapted for rural/agri users
- “Digital agriculture with empathy”
- Rich but clean
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Better density without clutter
- Elegant card system
- Smooth interaction patterns

The result should feel like a cross between:
- a modern gov-tech product
